​

A variation made with vanilla rather than chocolate in the batter is called a blonde brownie or blondie. The brownie was developed in the United States at the end of the 19th century and popularized in the U.S. and Canada during the first half of the 20th century. Brownies are a form of sheet cookie.

​

Ingredients

  • unsalted butter
  • best dark chocolate
  • plain flour
  • cocoa powder
  • white chocolate
  • milk chocolate
  • large eggs
  • golden caster sugar
